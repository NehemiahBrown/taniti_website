Unforgettable Experiences Await on Taniti Island

Taniti has a dining experience for everyone. Five island eateries dish up fresh-off-the-boat seafood prepared with rice and island spices. For those looking for something familiar, three American-style eateries dish up steaks, burgers, and home cooking. Two Pan-Asian eateries serve regional specialties based on the flavors of Thailand and Japan. From an island spin on things, or something more traditional, there is something for every palate.

Taniti features sandy and rocky shores that provide relaxation and views. White sandy shores ring Yellow Leaf Bay and provide the best for sunbathing, swimming, or walking in the evening. Some rocky shores yield stunning views and lonelier parts for the exploration of tide pools or photography. The beach has something for all, ranging from urban, family-oriented beaches near Taniti City to remote nature preserves in order to escape it all.
