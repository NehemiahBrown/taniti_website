Support the Island

Support Local Businesses

Choose to eat at locally owned restaurants, shop in family-run markets, and book tours with Tanitian guides. Supporting small businesses helps keep the island’s economy strong and benefits the local community directly.

Protect the Environment

Taniti’s natural beauty is a big part of what makes it special. Use reef-safe sunscreen, stay on marked trails, and avoid littering to help keep the beaches, rainforest, and ocean clean for everyone.

Celebrate the Culture

Take part in local traditions by attending festivals and exploring cultural sites. Buying handmade crafts and learning a few Tanitian words shows respect and helps preserve the island’s unique heritage.

Give Back

Visitors can support Taniti beyond tourism by donating to community programs or volunteering during their stay. Even small contributions help support education, environmental protection, and local arts.
